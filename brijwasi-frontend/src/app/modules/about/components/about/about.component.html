Our Story

Founded in 2020, Brijwasi Delight started with a simple mission: to deliver authentic Indian cuisine with the same love and care that goes into home cooking. What began as a small kitchen has grown into a trusted food delivery platform serving thousands of satisfied customers.

We believe that great food brings people together. Every dish we prepare is crafted with traditional recipes passed down through generations, using only the freshest and finest ingredients.
